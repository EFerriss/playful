About Elizabeth Ferriss

Hello! I'm a Winter 2018 Data Science Fellow at Insight.

My background is in Geology - I have a PhD from the University of Michigan and spent 6 years as a research scientist at the Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory at Columbia University, where I developed a python package to interpret geochemical data.

I love writing code to make awesome things that people can use.
